About Us

Our story started in 2011, in Year 1, with an iPad and iMovie.

We’re Harry and Noah — lifelong friends who discovered our passion for filmmaking before we even knew it could become a career. What began as making short films for fun quickly grew into something much bigger.

In 2016, we joined our primary school’s Film Club, where we got our first real taste of collaborative production. We worked as part of a team to create short commercials and skits, learning the foundations of storytelling, timing, and working behind the camera.

Through high school, we continued refining our craft together, completing four years of Multimedia studies and both achieving Band 6 major short films. During this time, we developed a strong understanding of film theory, cinematic techniques, camera operation, and professional editing software including Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects.

After graduation, we pursued our passion independently — Harry completing a Bachelor of Film in Brisbane, and Noah completing a Bachelor of Film in Byron Bay. Over three years, we gained hands-on experience through various industry roles and freelance projects, further sharpening our technical skills and creative direction.

Now, after earning our degrees and building real-world experience, we’ve come back together to launch our own freelance videography business.

From weddings to real estate and beyond, we approach every project with the same passion that started it all — a genuine love for storytelling and creating high-quality visuals that leave a lasting impact.

We’re proud of where we started, excited about where we’re going, and committed to delivering professional, cinematic content that exceeds expectations.
